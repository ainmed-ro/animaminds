## Executive summary

The Wave 1 assets for PMD_001 are solid, professional and aligned with the approved PMD. The Workbook is practical, the Facilitator Guide is delivery-ready, the Commercial Sheet is convincing, and the CPD Package is accreditation-ready with minor additions.

Main areas for improvement:
- Add a one-page next-day tool to the Workbook.
- Include exact deliverable wording and time checks in the Facilitator Guide.
- Add a clear "What you receive" section and next-day outcomes to the Commercial Sheet.
- Add a sample agenda and evaluation instruments to the CPD Package.

**Overall recommendation:** **REVISION REQUIRED** — minor, not major. After 3–4 small changes, the structure is ready for scaling across PMD_002–PMD_005.

## 1. Workbook.md

### Is the Workbook practical enough?

**Verdict:** Yes, with one gap.

**Strengths:**
- Covers every module with a matching worksheet.
- Includes the AI Audit, prompt template, scenario stations, verification checklist and 30-day plan.
- Uses Romanian context and roles.
- Contains fill-in fields and checklists, not just reading text.

**Gaps:**
- No standalone "next-day quick reference" one-pager.
- The AI Audit table has many columns; could overwhelm slower participants.
- No space for participants to paste or attach printed outputs.

**Score:** 8 / 10

### What can a participant actually use the next day?

A participant can use immediately:
- The prompt template (Section 4).
- The output verification checklist (Section 7).
- The 30-day action plan (Section 10).
- The ethical checklist (Section 9).

**Missing:** a single-page "AI Quick Start" summary combining the prompt structure + 3 safety questions.

**Score for next-day utility:** 7 / 10

### Are there sections that feel theoretical?

**Verdict:** Low risk.

- Section 2 (what AI is/is not) is reflective but necessary.
- Section 8 (AI vs. Human matrix) is conceptual but short and useful.
- No section is purely lecture-style.

**Score:** 8 / 10

### Are the exercises realistic?

**Verdict:** Yes.

- The email hook is realistic for all sectors.
- Sector Scenario Stations map directly to education, healthcare, public administration, business and NGO roles.
- Prompt Lab and AI Audit mirror real professional workflows.

**Suggested improvement:** Add one prompt example per sector in Section 6 to reduce blank-page anxiety.

**Score:** 8 / 10

## 2. Facilitator_Guide.md

### Is the Facilitator Guide detailed enough for another trainer to deliver the program?

**Verdict:** Yes, with minor additions.

**Strengths:**
- Clear facilitator role statement.
- Complete preparation checklist.
- Module-by-module scripts with timings.
- Assessment guide and troubleshooting.
- Online and Experience Edition adaptations included.

**Gaps:**
- Module scripts do not include exact phrases or transition lines.
- No timing checkpoints within long modules (e.g., Prompt Lab 60 min).
- No slide numbers mapped to script sections.
- Missing exact wording for the opening and closing.
- Ethical Dilemma Cards and Sector Scenario Station briefs are referenced but not included in the file.

**Score:** 7.5 / 10

### Specific improvements needed

1. Add an **opening script** (5 min) with welcome, promise, agenda and ground rules.
2. Add a **closing script** (5 min) with commitments, follow-up email preview and final promise.
3. Add **time checks** inside each module script (e.g., "At 20 min, call for first prompt iteration").
4. Add **slide references** in brackets next to each script section.
5. Include or attach the **Ethical Dilemma Cards** and **Sector Scenario Station briefs**.

- No explicit "What participants can do the next day" block.
- No pricing section or call-to-action detail.
- Missing testimonial placeholder or pilot result placeholder (acceptable for Wave 1).

**Score for school decision-makers:** 7.5 / 10  
**Score for hospital decision-makers:** 7.5 / 10  
**Score for company decision-makers:** 7 / 10  
**Score for public institution decision-makers:** 7.5 / 10

3. **Optional:** Add a short "Pilot results" or "Expected impact" paragraph.

## 4. CPD_Package.md

### Is the CPD Package accreditation-ready?

**Verdict:** Nearly ready.

**Strengths:**
- Complete LO ↔ assessment ↔ evidence mapping.
- Learning hours table with all formats.
- Facilitator qualifications are clear.
- Evaluation methodology covers T0, T1 and T2.
- Assessment rubric included.
- QA and ethics sections are strong.

**Gaps:**
- No sample agenda attached.
- No actual evaluation instruments (questionnaires, survey items).
- No certificate wording or completion criteria.
- Missing contact details placeholder is fine but should be flagged.
- No policy for late submissions or resits.

**Score:** 7.5 / 10

### What information is still missing?

1. **Sample agenda** by format (Online / On-site / Experience Edition).
2. **Evaluation instruments:**
   - Pre-program confidence questionnaire (5 items)
   - Satisfaction survey (5 items)
   - 30-day follow-up survey
3. **Completion criteria:**
   - Minimum attendance
   - Deliverable submission requirement
   - Reflection brief requirement
4. **Certificate wording** or certificate template reference.
5. **Data retention and privacy policy** for evaluation data.
6. **Appeals / resit policy** for participants who do not meet criteria.
7. **Accreditation body contact details** to be filled in.
