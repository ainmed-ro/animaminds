## 5. Facilitator qualifications

### Essential
- Demonstrated experience in training adults in leadership, management or organisational development.
- Ability to guide groups through ambiguity without imposing answers.
- Familiarity with prioritisation and decision-making frameworks.
- Capacity to keep the program focused on leadership tools, not therapy or consulting.

### Desirable
- Background in education, healthcare, public administration, business or NGOs.
- Experience delivering both online and in-person experiential learning.
- Completion of the AnimaMinds Busola Deciziilor facilitator induction.

### Facilitator ratio
- 1 facilitator per 20 participants.
- 2 facilitators for groups over 20 or where team-level decisions are worked.

**Rationale:** Decision-making and prioritisation sessions are primarily cognitive and framework-based, allowing the standard 1:20 ratio. A second facilitator supports team-level alignment work and breakout-room coverage.

## 6. Assessment and evaluation methodology

### 6.1 Completion criteria

Participants must:
- Attend at least 80% of contact hours.
- Submit Stakeholder Map + Change Map + Direction Pitch + 30-day action plan within 48 hours.
- Submit a reflection brief (150–250 words) within 48 hours.
- Complete the post-program satisfaction survey.

Remediation assignment available within 14 days if criteria not met.

### 6.2 Pre-program (T0)

**Clarity questionnaire (5 items, Likert 1–5)**
1. I can clearly distinguish between problems, decisions and directions.
2. I use structured frameworks for prioritisation.
3. I can identify the key stakeholders and criteria for a decision.
4. I can analyse change situations effectively.
5. I can communicate direction clearly to my team.

### 6.3 During program

- Facilitator observation checklist
- Peer feedback on direction pitches
- Module-end quick checks

### 6.4 Post-program (T1)

- Repeat clarity questionnaire
- Practical deliverable submission
- Reflection brief
- Satisfaction survey (5 items)

### 6.5 Follow-up (T2, 30 days)

- Short online survey: decisions made, priorities clarified, actions taken
- Optional second reflection brief
