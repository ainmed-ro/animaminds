## 5. Facilitator qualifications

### Essential
- Demonstrated experience in training adults in critical thinking, creativity, learning or cognitive skills.
- Strong facilitation skills for reflective, open-ended discussions.
- Ability to challenge assumptions without being dismissive.
- Capacity to keep the program focused on human cognition, not therapy, tools or strategy consulting.

### Desirable
- Background in education, healthcare, public administration, business or NGOs.
- Experience delivering both online and in-person experiential learning.
- Completion of the AnimaMinds Avantajul Uman facilitator induction.

### Facilitator ratio
- 1 facilitator per 18 participants.
- 2 facilitators for groups over 18 or where deep reflection is expected.

**Rationale:** Critical thinking and judgement exercises benefit from Socratic questioning and rich debrief. The 1:18 ratio ensures enough facilitator attention for reflective discussion.

## 6. Assessment and evaluation methodology

### 6.1 Completion criteria

Participants must:
- Attend at least 80% of contact hours.
- Submit Argument Anatomy + Bias Mitigation Checklist + revised Adaptability Plan + judgement case evaluation within 48 hours.
- Submit a reflection brief (150–250 words) within 48 hours.
- Complete the post-program satisfaction survey.

Remediation assignment available within 14 days if criteria not met.

### 6.2 Pre-program (T0)

**Self-assessment (5 items, Likert 1–5)**
1. I can identify assumptions in arguments.
2. I can recognise my own cognitive biases.
3. I can generate creative solutions under constraints.
4. I can adapt quickly when plans change.
5. I can explain what human judgement adds to AI outputs.

### 6.3 During program

- Facilitator observation checklist
- Peer feedback on case analyses
- Module-end quick checks

### 6.4 Post-program (T1)

- Repeat self-assessment
- Practical deliverable submission
- Reflection brief
- Satisfaction survey (5 items)

### 6.5 Follow-up (T2, 30 days)

- Short online survey: thinking habits practised, decisions improved, adaptations made
- Optional second reflection brief
