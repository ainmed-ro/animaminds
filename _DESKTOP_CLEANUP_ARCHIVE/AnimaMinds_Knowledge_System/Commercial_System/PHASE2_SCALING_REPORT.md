## 4. Structural consistency maintained

All 16 documents share:
- Same four-document taxonomy (Workbook, Facilitator Guide, Commercial Sheet, CPD Package).
- Same version numbering (v1.0 for scaled programs, v2.0 for PMD_001).
- Same delivery formats (Online, On-site, Open cohort, Experience Edition).
- Same learning hours and CPD point structure (7 / 8 / 7 / 12).
- Same module count and session length (6 modules, 6–7h on-site).
- Same assessment approach (practical deliverable + reflection brief + self-assessment).
- Same certificate wording structure.
- Same follow-up email cadence (Day 2, 7, 14, 30).
- Same emotional safety and scope boundaries (where relevant).

## 5. Deviations from template

| Deviation | Reason |
|---|---|
| **PMD_003 group size cap lowered to 20** | High-pressure role-play requires smaller groups for safety and practice depth |
| **PMD_003 has two facilitators for groups over 16** | Emotional safety and debrief intensity require more facilitator capacity |
| **PMD_005 group size cap is 20 for Experience Edition** | Deep reflection and creative immersion benefit from smaller cohorts |
| **PMD_004 on-site timing adjusted** | Modules 2 and 3 are longer (90 min each); agenda redistributed accordingly |
| **PMD_005 Facilitator Guide does not include scenario cards appendix** | Case study cards are embedded in the workbook and module scripts |
| **PMD_001 Commercial_Sheet_v2.md retained v2.0 numbering** | PMD_001 was the pilot; subsequent programs begin at v1.0 |

No structural redesigns were made. All deviations are operational, not architectural.

## 6. Quality observations

### Strengths
- All 16 documents are complete and aligned with their source PMDs.
- The four-document model scales cleanly across all five program categories.
- Sector adaptations are present in every workbook and commercial sheet.
- CPD packages include all required elements: LO mapping, agendas, rubrics, completion criteria, certificate wording.

### Risks
- Volume may reveal minor inconsistencies during a full cross-program review.
- PMD_003 and PMD_002 have overlapping safety protocols; a single consolidated protocol could be considered later.
- Some scenario cards in facilitator guides could be further enriched after pilot deliveries.
