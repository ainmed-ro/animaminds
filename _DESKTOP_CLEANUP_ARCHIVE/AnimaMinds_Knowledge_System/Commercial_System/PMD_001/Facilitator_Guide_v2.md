## 1. Facilitator role

You are not the "AI expert". You are a learning designer who helps participants:
- Reduce anxiety about AI.
- Use AI practically and ethically.
- Keep professional judgement and human control.

Your job is to create a safe, hands-on environment where participants try, fail, reflect and improve.

## 4. Opening script

**Time:** 5 minutes  
**Slide 1:** Title slide

> Bună dimineața / Bună ziua și bine ați venit la AI Fără Haos. Sunt [nume], facilitatorul vostru pentru următoarele ore. Înainte să începem, două lucruri importante.
>
> Primul: acest program nu este despre cod, despre tehnologie avansată sau despre a deveni experți în AI. Este despre a folosi AI-ul în activitatea voastră zilnică, în siguranță, fără haos.
>
> Al doilea: voi nu veți pleca cu liste lungi de teorie. Veți pleca cu un plan concret: ce taskuri delegați AI-ului, cum scrieți prompturi mai bune și cum verificați ceea ce produce AI-ul.
>
> Promisiunea programului este simplă: Folosește AI. Păstrează controlul.
>
> Reguli de bază astăzi: încercați fără teamă, greșiți în siguranță, întrebați oricând. Haideți să începem.

## 5. Module scripts

### Module 1 — Demistificarea AI-ului (60 min)

**Objective:** By the end of this module, participants can explain what generative AI can and cannot do and can identify basic ethical and privacy guardrails.

**Slides 2–6**

**Hook (20 min):**
- 0–3 min: Distribute prompt: *„Scrieți un email scurt de scuză către un părinte/coleg/pacient/client."*
- 3–8 min: Participants use a free AI tool.
- 8–15 min: Pairs compare AI draft vs. what they would write.
- 15–20 min: Whole-group debrief.

**Discussion questions:**
- What felt easy?
- What felt wrong or generic?
- What would you not delegate to AI?

**Content walkthrough (30 min):**
- Slides 7–10: Define generative AI in one sentence.
- Slides 11–13: Distinguish generative vs. predictive vs. analytic AI.
- Slides 14–17: List 3 common myths and the reality.
- Slides 18–20: Privacy rule — no personal, confidential or sensitive data in public tools.

**Time check at 40 min:** Hook and content must be complete. If behind, shorten myth discussion.

**Consolidation (10 min):**
- Participants complete Workbook Section 2.
- Share one AI strength and one limitation in pairs.

**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 2 completed.

---

### Module 2 — Identificarea taskurilor potrivite (60 min)

**Objective:** By the end of this module, participants can identify at least 5 repetitive tasks suitable for AI acceleration and know when NOT to use AI.

**Slides 21–25**

**Activity: AI Audit personal (40 min)**
- 0–5 min: Introduce the AI Audit worksheet (Workbook Section 3).
- 5–25 min: Participants list 10 tasks and classify each using:
  - repetitive
  - structured
  - text-based
  - allows fast feedback
- 25–35 min: Mark which tasks can be delegated to AI.
- 35–40 min: Volunteers share one task they will delegate and one they will keep human-only.

**Debrief questions:**
- Which tasks appeared most often across the group?
- Which tasks did nobody want to delegate? Why?
- What is one task that looks simple but should stay human-only?

**Content (20 min):**
- Slides 26–28: Tasks AI handles well.
- Slides 29–32: Tasks AI should NOT handle.

**Time check at 40 min:** All worksheets should be mostly complete.

**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 3 completed.

---

### Module 3 — Prompting ca abilitate (90 min)

**Objective:** By the end of this module, participants can use role, context, task, format, constraints and iteration to improve prompts.

**Slides 33–40**

**Mini-lesson (20 min):**
- Slides 33–36: Prompt anatomy — Role → Context → Task → Format → Constraints → Examples.
- Slides 37–40: Show one weak prompt and one strong prompt side by side.

**Activity: Prompt Lab (60 min)**
- 0–5 min: Form pairs. Each pair chooses one task from their AI Audit.
- 5–15 min: Write first prompt and run it.
- 15–20 min: Evaluate output using the checklist (Workbook Section 5).
- 20–35 min: Rewrite prompt with one added element.
- 35–50 min: Run again and compare.
- 50–60 min: Pairs share what one change made the biggest difference.

**Time check at 50 min:** Second iteration should be complete. If behind, shorten sharing to 5 min.

**Debrief (10 min):**
- What did the AI still get wrong?
- How much depends on the prompt vs. the tool?

**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 5 completed.

---

### Module 4 — Aplicații practice pe roluri (90 min)

**Objective:** By the end of this module, each participant has drafted at least one AI-assisted work asset relevant to their role.

**Slides 41–45**

**Activity: Sector Scenario Stations (70 min)**
- 0–5 min: Set up five stations or breakout rooms:
  - Education
  - Healthcare
  - Public Administration
  - Business
  - NGO
- 5–10 min: Participants move to the station matching their role.
- 10–15 min: Read the Sector Scenario Brief (Appendix B).
- 15–40 min: Write a prompt, generate output, edit it.
- 40–55 min: Prepare a 1-minute share.
- 55–70 min: Each station presents one example.

**Time check at 40 min:** Output must be generated. If behind, skip the 1-minute prep.

**Sharing and feedback (20 min):**
- Each station shares one example.
- Group gives feedback on:
  - Prompt quality
  - Output usefulness
  - Human edits
  - Safety and ethics

**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 6 completed.

---

### Module 5 — Verificare și editare (60 min)

**Objective:** By the end of this module, participants can critically evaluate AI outputs and adapt them to their own voice and context.

**Slides 46–52**

**Mini-lesson (20 min):**
- Slides 46–48: Common output risks — hallucinations, outdated information, generic tone, bias, missing context.
- Slides 49–52: Three-step editing — check facts, adjust tone, add specifics.

**Activity: AI vs. Human (30 min)**
- 0–5 min: Provide an AI-generated draft with intentional weaknesses.
- 5–20 min: Participants mark what needs to change and rewrite one paragraph.
- 20–30 min: Volunteers share before/after versions.

**Time check at 30 min:** Rewrites must be done.

**Debrief (10 min):**
- What is the difference between a usable draft and a final deliverable?
- What is your personal "human stamp"?

**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 7 completed.

---

### Module 6 — Plan personal de utilizare (30 min)

**Objective:** By the end of this module, participants have a concrete 30-day plan.

**Slides 53–55**

**Activity (20 min):**
- 0–5 min: Introduce Workbook Section 10.
- 5–15 min: Participants complete the 30-Day Action Plan.
- 15–20 min: Identify a "Buddy AI" and the first task for tomorrow.

**Closing (10 min):**
- Each participant shares one commitment in one sentence.
- Remind them of the follow-up email sequence.
- End with the promise: *„Folosește AI. Păstrează controlul."*

**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 10 completed.

## 6. Closing script

**Time:** 5 minutes  
**Slide 56:** Closing slide

> Am ajuns la final. În următoarele 30 de zile, veți primi patru emailuri de la noi: unul mâine, unul peste o săptămână, unul peste două săptămâni și unul peste o lună.
>
> Scopul lor este simplu: să vă ajute să nu uitați ce ați învățat azi.
>
> Înainte să plecați, am trei întrebări:
> 1. Care este primul lucru pe care îl veți încerca mâine cu AI?
> 2. Ce nu veți delega niciodată AI-ului?
> 3. Cu cine veți împărtăși progresul?
>
> Aduceți-vă aminte: AI-ul este un instrument. Voi sunteți autorul, responsabilul și decidentul.
>
> Folosește AI. Păstrează controlul. Mulțumesc.

## 7. Assessment guide

### 7.1 Practical deliverable

Each participant submits one AI-assisted work asset relevant to their role.

Use the PMD rubric:

| Criterion | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Acceptable (2) | Needs revision (1) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Role relevance | Directly supports real work | Relevant to role | Generic but usable | Not relevant |
| Prompt quality | Includes role, context, format, constraints | 3 of 4 elements | 1–2 elements | Vague |
| Human editing | Fully adapted to voice and context | Minor edits needed | Noticeably generic | No personalisation |
| Accuracy & safety | Facts verified; no sensitive data; checklist followed | Minor issues | One significant risk | Unverified or unsafe |
| Reflection | Clear 30-day plan with first step | Plan present but vague | Incomplete | No plan |

### 7.2 Observation checklist

During the session, observe:
- [ ] Participants can log in to at least one AI tool.
- [ ] Participants complete the AI Audit with 5+ tasks.
- [ ] Participants iterate at least one prompt.
- [ ] Participants can explain the privacy rule.
- [ ] Participants complete the 30-Day Action Plan.

### 7.3 Success indicators
- 100% of participants submit a practical deliverable.
- 90% of participants report increased confidence in using AI.
- 80% of participants identify at least 3 tasks they will delegate to AI within 30 days.

## 10. Troubleshooting

| Issue | Response |
|---|---|
| AI tool is slow or unavailable | Switch to backup tool. Use offline prompt-writing exercises. |
| Participant enters sensitive data | Stop immediately. Use as live learning moment. Refer to privacy checklist. |
| AI produces false information | Use it in Module 5. Ask group to identify error and correct it. |
| Participant is resistant or fearful | Acknowledge concern. Use "AI as calculator, not replacement" framing. |
| Participant wants advanced technical content | Redirect to program scope. Offer further resources. |
| Group finishes early | Add a bonus scenario or deeper prompt iteration. |
| Group falls behind | Shorten debrief; move optional exercises to workbook. |

## 12. Safety and ethics

- Never require participants to share personal data with AI.
- Remind participants to check their organisation's AI policy.
- If a participant shares sensitive information by mistake, treat it as a learning moment, not a punishment.
- Keep the tone supportive, not alarmist.

## Appendix A — Ethical Dilemma Cards

Print, cut and shuffle. Use 1–2 cards in Module 1 or Module 5.

### Card 1: Date personale
**Situație:** Un coleg vrea să introducă date personale ale unui client într-un tool AI pentru a genera un răspuns personalizat.
**Întrebare:** Ce îi spui? Ce alternativă propui?

### Card 2: Conținut medical
**Situație:** AI-ul generează o recomandare medicală specifică pentru un pacient fictiv într-un scenariu de training.
**Întrebare:** Este acceptabil să folosești textul așa cum este? Ce trebuie modificat?

### Card 3: Raport intern
**Situație:** Trebuie să rezumi un raport intern care conține cifre confidențiale ale companiei.
**Întrebare:** Cum folosești AI fără a expune datele?

### Card 4: Evaluarea elevilor
**Situație:** AI-ul îți propune note pentru lucrările elevilor pe baza unui model generic.
**Întrebare:** Care sunt riscurile? Cine este responsabil?

### Card 5: Marketing agresiv
**Situație:** AI-ul generează un email de marketing care sună prea persuasiv și ar putea induce în eroare clienții.
**Întrebare:** Ce modificări faci? Ce verifici?

### Card 6: Propunere de grant
**Situație:** AI-ul compune o parte dintr-o cerere de finanțare pentru un ONG. Datele despre beneficiari sunt reale.
**Întrebare:** Ce poate rămâne și ce trebuie editat sau eliminat?

## Appendix B — Sector Scenario Briefs

### Education Station
**Scenario:** You have just finished marking a set of essays. You need to give 5 students individual, constructive feedback without using their real names.
**Task:** Use AI to generate 5 short feedback paragraphs, then personalise them.
**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 6 — Education.

### Healthcare Station
**Scenario:** A patient needs clear, calm instructions about preparing for a routine blood test. No personal data can be included.
**Task:** Use AI to draft a short explanation, then review it for tone and safety.
**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 6 — Healthcare.

### Public Administration Station
**Scenario:** Your office needs a public announcement about opening a new community centre.
**Task:** Use AI to draft the announcement, then edit for accessibility, accuracy and institutional tone.
**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 6 — Public Administration.

### Business Station
**Scenario:** You need a follow-up email after a client meeting. Terms are not yet finalised.
**Task:** Use AI to draft the email, then adjust for brand voice and relationship stage.
**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 6 — Business.

### NGO Station
**Scenario:** You need a short donor update about recent environmental project results. No beneficiary personal data can be shared.
**Task:** Use AI to draft the email, then check for impact, ethics and a single call to action.
**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 6 — NGO.
