# Phase 2 Quality Audit

**Scope:** Review all 25 program assets across the 5 AnimaMinds programs  
**Assets reviewed:**
- 5 PMDs (PMD_001_AI_Fara_Haos_v2.md, PMD_002_Conversatii_care_Conteaza_v2.md, PMD_003_Calm_sub_Presiune_v2.md, PMD_004_Busola_Deciziilor_v2.md, PMD_005_Avantajul_Uman_v2.md)
- 5 Workbooks (PMD_001 v2.0, PMD_002–PMD_005 v1.0)
- 5 Facilitator Guides (PMD_001 v2.0, PMD_002–PMD_005 v1.0)
- 5 Commercial Sheets (PMD_001 v2.0, PMD_002–PMD_005 v1.0)
- 5 CPD Packages (PMD_001 v2.0, PMD_002–PMD_005 v1.0)

**Audit date:** 2026-07-12  
**Auditor:** Cascade

---

## 1. Executive summary

The Phase 2 asset library is **substantially complete and internally consistent**, but several fixes are required before the portfolio is ready for pilot delivery. The main issues are operational inconsistencies (group sizes, facilitator ratios, duplicate safety content) and the risk of confusion caused by superseded Wave 1 v1.0 PMD_001 files coexisting with approved v2.0 files.

**Overall recommendation:** **REVISION REQUIRED** — minor. After 8–10 targeted fixes, the portfolio can be approved for pilot delivery.

## 3. Strengths

1. **Validated template architecture.** The four-document model from PMD_001 scales cleanly.
2. **Clear program boundaries.** Each program stays in its lane; overlap is explicitly managed.
3. **Sector adaptations.** All five sectors are represented in workbooks, commercial sheets and scenario materials.
4. **CPD accreditation readiness.** Core CPD elements — LO mapping, hours, agendas, rubrics, certificate — are present.
5. **Consistent participant experience.** Same module count, follow-up cadence and deliverable structure across programs.
6. **Practical orientation.** Workbooks contain exercises, templates and one-pagers suitable for immediate use.
7. **Safety awareness.** Emotional safety protocols and referral guidance are included where needed.

---

## 4. Weaknesses

1. **PMD_001 v1.0 files coexist with approved v2.0 files.** Risk of confusion or accidental use of superseded assets.
2. **Group-size statements are imprecise.** PMD_003 Commercial Sheet uses a generic range that does not match its PMD.
3. **Facilitator ratios vary without documented rationale.** PMD_002 1:18, PMD_003 1:16, others 1:20.
4. **Emotional safety protocol duplicated.** Maintenance risk between PMD_002 and PMD_003.
5. **CPD Packages lack data-retention and privacy policy.** Accreditors typically require this.
6. **PMD_001 CPD Package v2 lacks explicit remediation/appeals wording.** PMD_002–PMD_005 have it.
7. **Some facilitator guides lack appendices.** PMD_005 Facilitator Guide does not include a scenario cards appendix (case studies are embedded in workbook).
8. **Commercial sheets could include impact/ROI language** for specific buyer types.
9. **PMDs do not list self-study hours** while CPD Packages do; potential disconnect with accreditors.
10. **No cross-program consistency review record** exists before this audit.

## 6. Approval recommendation

**CONDITIONAL APPROVAL FOR PILOT DELIVERY.**

The Phase 2 commercialization asset library is **ready for pilot delivery after Priority 1–5 fixes are applied.** Priority 6–10 are enhancements that can be completed during or after the first pilot.

Recommended pilot sequence:
1. **PMD_001 AI Fără Haos** — highest confidence; validates end-to-end delivery.

3. **PMD_002 Conversații care Contează** — validates emotionally sensitive content.
4. **PMD_003 Calm sub Presiune** — validates high-pressure scenarios.
5. **PMD_005 Avantajul Uman** — validates thinking-skills content.
