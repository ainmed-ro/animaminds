# Pilot Feedback Collection Kit

**Status:** READY FOR PILOT DELIVERY  
**Scope:** Collect structured feedback before, during and after a pilot program  
**Applies to:** PMD_001–PMD_005  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last updated:** 2026-07-12

---

## 1. Purpose

This kit provides ready-to-use instruments for collecting participant and facilitator feedback during pilot deliveries. Use the same instruments across all pilots to enable comparison.

---

## 2. T0 — Pre-program survey

Send 48 hours before delivery.

### Confidence / baseline (Likert 1–5)

1. I feel confident in the topic of this program.
2. I understand how this topic applies to my daily work.
3. I have clear expectations about what I will learn.

### Context

- My role / sector: ___________________________
- My experience level with this topic: [ ] Beginner [ ] Intermediate [ ] Advanced
- One thing I hope to gain from this program: ___________________________

---

## 3. T1 — Post-program survey

Send within 24 hours after delivery.

### Satisfaction (Likert 1–5)

1. The program met my expectations.
2. The facilitator explained concepts clearly.
3. The activities were practical and relevant.
4. I feel more confident in the topic after the program.
5. I would recommend this program to a colleague.

### Learning outcomes (Likert 1–5)

For each program-specific learning outcome, ask:
- I can [insert LO].

### Practical application

6. What is the first thing you will apply from this program?
7. What barrier, if any, might prevent you from applying what you learned?

### Open feedback

8. What worked well?
9. What could be improved?
10. Any other comments?

---

## 4. T2 — 30-day follow-up survey

Send 30 days after delivery.

### Application (Likert 1–5)

1. I have applied at least one thing I learned in this program.
2. I have noticed a positive impact on my work.
3. The follow-up emails were useful.

### Concrete results

4. How many times have you used the skills/tools from this program?  
   [ ] 0 [ ] 1–2 [ ] 3–5 [ ] 6+
5. One concrete result or example: ___________________________
6. What challenge have you encountered? ___________________________

### Net Promoter Score

7. How likely are you to recommend this program to a colleague? (0–10)

---

## 5. During-program pulse checks

Use at the end of each module or after major exercises.

### 3-question pulse

1. What was most useful in this module? (1 sentence)
2. What was unclear or missing?
3. What should we change before the next pilot?

### Emoji check

Ask participants to react with one emoji:
- 😊 I understand and can apply this
- 🤔 I mostly understand but need more practice
- 😕 I am confused or need more explanation

---

## 7. Data handling

- Store responses securely and anonymously where possible.
- Follow the data retention policy in each program's CPD Package.
- Share only aggregated findings with stakeholders.
- Do not share individual feedback without consent.
