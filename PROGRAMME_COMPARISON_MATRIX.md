## Key Takeaways

- **Duration:** All programmes are offered in the same three formats. Online Live and Onsite durations are 6h–6.5h contact time; Experience Edition is 10h contact time over 2 days.
- **Total Learning Hours:** 7h or 7.5h for standard formats; 12h for Experience Edition.
- **CPD Credits:** 7 or 8 for standard formats; 12 for Experience Edition.
- **Group Size:** Online Live runs with 15–30 participants; Onsite runs with 15–30 participants; Experience Edition runs with 20–30 participants.
- **Participant Package:** Identical across all five programmes — workbook PDF, one-pager, 30-day action plan, certificate of completion, optional 4-week follow-up email sequence.
- **Audience:** All programmes address professionals, managers and teachers, with Calm sub Presiune specifically including medical staff and public administration, and Busola Deciziilor focusing on decision-makers and team leaders.
