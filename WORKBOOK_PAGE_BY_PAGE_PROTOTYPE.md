## **PARTICIPANT JOURNEY SUMMARY**

### **Engagement Flow:**
1. **Professional Entry** (Pages 1-2) - Ownership and motivation
2. **Orientation** (Pages 3-7) - Understanding and preparation
3. **Skill Building** (Pages 8-22) - Progressive learning with practice
4. **Application** (Pages 23-24) - Real-world scenarios and reflection
5. **Closure** (Pages 25-26) - Celebration and future planning
6. **Support** (Pages 27-28) - Ongoing resources and notes

### **Learning Progression:**
- **Foundation** → **Skills** → **Application** → **Commitment**
- **Theory** → **Practice** → **Reflection** → **Action**
- **Individual** → **Interactive** → **Real-world** → **Personal**

### **Participant Actions per Page:**
- **Passive:** 4 pages (cover, contents, overview, structure)
- **Active:** 24 pages (writing, reflecting, practicing, planning)
- **Support:** 2 pages (resources, notes)

### **Key Experience Touchpoints:**
- **Page 2:** Personal connection through postponed conversation
- **Page 9:** First skill practice with sentence reframing
- **Page 16:** Complete feedback script creation
- **Page 18:** Difficult conversation scenario planning
- **Page 22:** Personal 30-day action plan
- **Page 24:** Signed commitment to change
