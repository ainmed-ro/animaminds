# Internal Review: PMD_005_Avantajul_Uman

**Review date:** 2026-07-12  
**Reviewer:** Cascade  
**Status:** Draft — PMD_005 not modified; review only

---

## Executive summary

PMD_005_Avantajul_Uman is a strong first draft that follows the approved master template. It focuses on critical thinking, adaptability, creativity and judgement while avoiding overlap with PMD_001 (AI tools), PMD_002 (communication mechanics), PMD_003 (conflict/de-escalation) and PMD_004 (leadership/decision-making). The sector adaptations are present and the tone is aligned.

Main areas for improvement: a more explicit boundary matrix, a stronger human–AI complementarity framing, more concrete creativity and adaptability exercises, and a clearer "next-day" application tool.

**Overall readiness:** 7.3 / 10 — good draft, minor refinements needed before approval.

## 3. What can a participant do the next day after completing the program?

**Verdict:** Clear but could be more explicit.

A participant can:
- Deconstruct an argument into claims, evidence and assumptions.
- Spot at least one cognitive bias in a real situation.
- Use SCAMPER or a constraint prompt to generate ideas.
- Pause before accepting an AI-generated recommendation.
- Start a 30-day thinking-habit plan.

**Suggested improvement:** Add a "Human Edge Checklist" one-pager for immediate use.

**Score:** 7 / 10
