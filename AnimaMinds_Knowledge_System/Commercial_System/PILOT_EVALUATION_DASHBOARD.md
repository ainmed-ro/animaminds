## 3. Key metrics

### Participation

| Metric | How to measure | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Registration vs. attendance | Attended / Registered | ≥ 80% |
| Attendance vs. completion | Completed / Attended | ≥ 90% |
| Deliverable submission rate | Submitted / Attended | ≥ 90% |

### Satisfaction

| Metric | How to measure | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Overall satisfaction | T1 average of "met expectations" | ≥ 4.0 / 5 |
| Facilitator clarity | T1 average | ≥ 4.2 / 5 |
| Practical relevance | T1 average | ≥ 4.0 / 5 |
| Net Promoter Score | T2 question (0–10) | ≥ 30 |

### Learning outcomes

| Metric | How to measure | Target |
|---|---|---|
| LO confidence gain | T1 vs. T0 average per LO | ≥ +0.5 points |
| Deliverable quality | Rubric average | ≥ 3.0 / 4 |
| Reflection depth | Rubric criterion | ≥ 3.0 / 4 |

### Application

| Metric | How to measure | Target |
|---|---|---|
| 30-day application rate | T2 "applied at least one thing" | ≥ 70% |
| Reported impact | T2 open question themes | Documented |
| Follow-up email open rate | Email analytics | ≥ 50% |

## 6. Definitions

- **Completion rate:** Participants who attended at least 80% and submitted required deliverables.
- **Avg satisfaction:** Average of all T1 satisfaction items.
- **NPS:** Net Promoter Score = % promoters (9–10) − % detractors (0–6).
- **Apply rate:** % of T2 respondents who report applying at least one learning.
