## 5. Facilitator qualifications

### Essential
- Demonstrated experience in training adults in communication, soft skills or team development.
- Strong facilitation skills for emotionally sensitive topics.
- Ability to maintain program scope and refer beyond-scope issues appropriately.
- Familiarity with feedback, active listening and psychological safety frameworks.

### Desirable
- Background in education, healthcare, public administration, business or NGOs.
- Experience delivering both online and in-person experiential learning.
- Completion of the AnimaMinds Conversații care Contează facilitator induction.

### Facilitator ratio
- 1 facilitator per 18 participants.
- 2 facilitators for groups over 18 or where trust issues exist.

**Rationale:** Communication and feedback work can surface personal or team sensitivity. A lower ratio than standard ensures adequate debrief support and emotional safety.

## 6. Assessment and evaluation methodology

### 6.1 Completion criteria

Participants must:
- Attend at least 80% of contact hours.
- Submit a completed feedback script + 4-sentence message within 48 hours.
- Submit a reflection brief (150–250 words) within 48 hours.
- Complete the post-program satisfaction survey.

Remediation assignment available within 14 days if criteria not met.

### 6.2 Pre-program (T0)

**Confidence questionnaire (5 items, Likert 1–5)**
1. I feel confident giving constructive feedback.
2. I can listen actively when emotions are high.
3. I can express disagreement without damaging the relationship.
4. I can align a team around a decision through dialogue.
5. I know how to create psychological safety in conversations.

### 6.3 During program

- Facilitator observation checklist
- Peer feedback on feedback scripts
- Module-end quick checks

### 6.4 Post-program (T1)

- Repeat confidence questionnaire
- Practical deliverable submission
- Reflection brief
- Satisfaction survey (5 items)

### 6.5 Follow-up (T2, 30 days)

- Short online survey: conversations initiated, feedback given, alignment observed
- Optional second reflection brief

## 7. Assessment rubric

### Feedback Script Rubric

| Criterion | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Acceptable (2) | Needs revision (1) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Clarity | Unambiguous and specific | Minor ambiguity | Vague or general | Confusing |
| Connection | Builds trust and openness | Mostly respectful | Neutral or distant | Hostile or cold |
| Actionability | Clear request or next step | Next step implied | No clear next step | No request |
| Psychological safety | Recipient's perspective acknowledged | Some acknowledgment | Little acknowledgment | Dismissive |
| Reflection | Clear insight and planned change | Insight present | Surface reflection | No reflection |

### Active Listening Rubric

| Criterion | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Acceptable (2) | Needs revision (1) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Paraphrase | Captures content and feeling | Captures content | Partial capture | Misrepresents |
| Open questions | Expands understanding | Mostly open | Mix | Dominates with statements |
| Validation | Acknowledges emotions | Acknowledges briefly | Minimal | Ignores emotions |
| Summarising | Pulls themes together | Summarises main points | Partial | None |
| Presence | Supports listening non-verbally | Mostly present | Distracted at times | Interrupting |
