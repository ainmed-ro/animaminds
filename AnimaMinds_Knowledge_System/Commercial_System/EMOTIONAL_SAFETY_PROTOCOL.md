# Emotional Safety Protocol

**Version:** 1.0  
**Applies to:** PMD_002 Conversații care Contează, PMD_003 Calm sub Presiune, and any other AnimaMinds program where emotionally sensitive content may emerge.

---

## Purpose

Protect participants and maintain program scope when emotionally charged content emerges during delivery. This protocol is **not** a substitute for therapy, counselling or professional mental health support.

---

## If a participant becomes visibly upset

1. **Pause** the activity calmly.
2. **Acknowledge** the emotion without pressuring the person to continue:
   > *„Văd că acest subiect atinge ceva personal. Nu trebuie să continuăm în rol."*
3. **Offer options:**
   - Continue as an observer.
   - Take a short break.
   - Speak privately with the facilitator.
4. **Do not** offer counselling, diagnosis or advice beyond the program scope.
5. **Refer** to appropriate support if needed (HR, EAP, therapist, manager).
6. **Return** the group to the activity or move to a less intense exercise.

---

## If a participant discloses trauma, mental health crisis or abuse

1. **Thank** them for trusting the group.
2. **State clearly:** *„Acest program nu oferă terapie sau consiliere. Vă recomand să discutați cu un specialist."*
3. **Offer** a private follow-up conversation to identify appropriate referral options.
4. **Document** the referral offer and any action taken.

---

## Facilitator self-care

- Debrief emotionally demanding sessions with a co-facilitator or supervisor.
- Avoid taking responsibility for participants' personal outcomes.
- Take breaks between consecutive deliveries.

---

## Scope boundaries

- This protocol supports **communication skills** (PMD_002) and **conflict/pressure management skills** (PMD_003).
- It does **not** address clinical treatment, mediation of active conflicts, or legal disputes.
- Facilitators must refer participants to qualified professionals when issues exceed program scope.
