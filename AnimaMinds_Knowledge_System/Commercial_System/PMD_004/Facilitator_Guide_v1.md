## 1. Facilitator role

You are not a strategy consultant or executive coach. You are a learning facilitator who helps participants:
- Find clarity and direction.
- Use prioritisation and decision-making frameworks.
- Lead change without imposing answers.

Your job is to guide groups through ambiguity, not to solve their problems for them.

## 5. Module scripts

### Module 1 — Ce înseamnă claritatea (60 min)

**Objective:** Participants can distinguish problem, decision and direction.

**Slides 2–6**

**Hook (20 min):**
- Participants write one decision or direction they have been avoiding.
- Share in pairs what makes it unclear.
- Introduce: clarity is an action, not a feeling.

**Content (30 min):**
- Slides 7–10: Problemă vs. decizie vs. direcție.
- Slides 11–15: Cost of delay and cost of wrong decision.
- Slides 16–20: When clarity means "we don't know yet".

**Time check at 40 min:** Hook and content complete.

**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 2.

---

### Module 2 — Instrumente de prioritizare (90 min)

**Objective:** Participants can apply prioritisation frameworks.

**Slides 21–32**

**Mini-lesson (20 min):**
- Slides 21–25: Eisenhower matrix.
- Slides 26–30: Impact/effort matrix.
- Slides 31–32: MUST/SHOULD/COULD.

**Activity: Priority Sort (60 min)**
- 0–10 min: Participants list 5–10 priorities.
- 10–25 min: Apply Eisenhower.
- 25–40 min: Apply impact/effort.
- 40–55 min: Apply MUST/SHOULD/COULD.
- 55–60 min: Debrief top 3 priorities.

**Time check at 45 min:** Two frameworks applied.

**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 3.

---

### Module 3 — Cadre de luare a deciziilor (90 min)

**Objective:** Participants can use a structured decision process.

**Slides 33–44**

**Mini-lesson (25 min):**
- Slides 33–38: 6-step decision process.
- Slides 39–44: Decision criteria and individual vs. team decisions.

**Activity: Decision Lab (55 min)**
- 0–10 min: Choose a real decision.
- 10–25 min: Walk through the 6 steps.
- 25–40 min: Identify stakeholders and criteria.
- 40–55 min: Pair share and feedback.

**Time check at 40 min:** 6 steps complete.

**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 4 and 5.

---

### Module 4 — Schimbarea și rezistența (60 min)

**Objective:** Participants can analyse a change situation and resistance.

**Slides 45–52**

**Mini-lesson (15 min):**
- Slides 45–48: Change model — present → desired → gap → steps.
- Slides 49–52: Common sources of resistance.

**Activity: Change Map (40 min)**
- 0–5 min: Introduce Change Map template.
- 5–25 min: Complete in pairs.
- 25–40 min: Debrief resistance and responses.

**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 6.

---

### Module 5 — Comunicarea direcției (60 min)

**Objective:** Participants can deliver a clear direction pitch.

**Slides 53–58**

**Mini-lesson (15 min):**
- Slides 53–55: Direction message structure.
- Slides 56–58: Practice tips.

**Activity: Direction Pitch (40 min)**
- 0–10 min: Complete Direction Pitch template.
- 10–40 min: Deliver in triads or breakouts (3 minutes each).

**Debrief (5 min):**
- What made a pitch compelling?
- What was still unclear?

**Deliverable:** Workbook Section 7.

---

### Module 6 — Planul de 30 de zile (30 min)

**Objective:** Participants create a concrete action plan.

**Slides 59–61**

**Activity (20 min):**
- Complete Workbook Section 9.
- Pair share first step.

**Closing (10 min):**
- Each participant states one commitment.
- Remind of follow-up emails.
- End with promise: *„Claritate și direcție atunci când lucrurile par neclare."*

## 6. Closing script

**Time:** 5 minutes  
**Slide 62:** Closing slide

> Am ajuns la final. În următoarele 30 de zile veți primi emailuri care vă ajută să mențineți claritatea.
>
> Trei întrebări:
> 1. Care este prima decizie sau direcție pe care o veți clarifica?
> 2. Ce cadru veți folosi?
> 3. Cui veți comunica direcția?
>
> Claritatea nu este un sentiment. Este un obicei.
>
> Claritate și direcție atunci când lucrurile par neclare. Mulțumesc.

## 7. Assessment guide

### 7.1 Practical deliverable

Each participant submits Stakeholder Map + Change Map + Direction Pitch + 30-day action plan.

Use the PMD rubric:

| Criterion | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Acceptable (2) | Needs revision (1) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Problem clarity | Decision or direction clearly named | Mostly clear | Vague | Missing |
| Framework fit | Chosen framework fits situation | Reasonable fit | Forced fit | No framework |
| Stakeholder / criteria map | Key stakeholders and criteria identified with rationale | Most identified | Some identified | None |
| Change analysis | Change Map shows clear present/desired state, gap, steps and resistance | Mostly complete | Some elements missing | Incomplete |
| Direction pitch | Clear, compelling and actionable | Mostly clear | Vague | Confusing |
| Action plan | 30-day plan has priorities, owners and first steps | Plan present | Incomplete | No plan |

### 7.2 Success indicators
- 100% submit all four deliverables.
- 90% report increased clarity on at least one priority.
- 80% identify at least 2 concrete actions within 30 days.

## 9. Troubleshooting

| Issue | Response |
|---|---|
| Participant says „nu am decizii de luat" | Reframe: direction, priority and trade-off are decisions. |
| Group fixates on one problem | Use 6-step process to broaden or narrow frame. |
| Hidden conflict about priorities | Use impact/effort matrix visibly; do not mediate personal conflict. |
| Participant wants to revisit past decisions | Acknowledge, redirect to forward-looking action. |
| Online participants disengage | Use shorter activities, polls, breakouts. |

## 10. Follow-up email sequence

**Day 2 — First priority:**
> Subiect: Prima prioritate pe care o vei clarifica  
> Conținut: Care este prima decizie sau direcție pe care o vei aborda?

**Day 7 — Reflection:**
> Subiect: Ce a devenit mai clar?  
> Conținut: Ai folosit vreun cadru? Ce te-a ajutat?

**Day 14 — Resource:**
> Subiect: Cum comunici o direcție într-un email  
> Conținut: șablon scurt pentru mesajul de direcție.

**Day 30 — Outcome:**
> Subiect: Planul tău de 30 de zile — rezultate  
> Conținut: Survey scurt: ce decizii ai luat? Ce impact ai observat?
