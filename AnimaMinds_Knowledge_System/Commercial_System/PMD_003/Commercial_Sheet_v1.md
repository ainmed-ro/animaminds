## What participants will be able to do

1. Recognise physiological and behavioural signs of escalation.
2. Apply at least 3 emotional self-regulation techniques.
3. Analyse conflict using positions, interests and needs.
4. Choose an appropriate de-escalation strategy.
5. Respond with calm, clear, professional messages under pressure.
6. Set and maintain boundaries under pressure.
7. Create a personal calm-under-pressure plan.
8. Identify when referral to HR, mediation or support is needed.

## FAQ for clients

**Q: Is this therapy or mediation?**
A: No. The program teaches practical self-regulation and de-escalation skills. Deeper issues are referred appropriately.

**Q: Is role-play mandatory?**
A: No. Written and observer alternatives are available.

**Q: What if a participant has a real conflict with a colleague?**
A: The program builds individual skills. Active workplace conflicts may need HR or mediation.

**Q: What follow-up is included?**
A: A 30-day email sequence with reflection prompts and resources.
