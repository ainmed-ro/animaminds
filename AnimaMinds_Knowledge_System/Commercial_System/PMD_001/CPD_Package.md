## 4. Facilitator qualifications

### Essential
- Demonstrated experience in training adults in a professional context.
- Practical experience using generative AI tools in their own work.
- Ability to explain technical concepts in non-technical language.
- Familiarity with data privacy and AI ethics basics.

### Desirable
- Background in education, healthcare, public administration or business (to match sector adaptations).
- Experience delivering both online and in-person training.
- Completion of the AnimaMinds AI Fără Haos facilitator induction.

### Facilitator ratio
- 1 facilitator per 20 participants.
- 2 facilitators for groups over 20.

---

## 5. Assessment and evaluation methodology

### Pre-program (T0)
- Confidence questionnaire (5 items, Likert 1–5)
- Self-assessment of current AI use

### During program
- Facilitator observation checklist
- Participant worksheet completion
- Module-end quick checks

### Post-program (T1, within 48h)
- Repeat confidence questionnaire
- Practical deliverable submission
- Reflection brief (150–250 words)
- Satisfaction survey (5 items)

### Follow-up (T2, 30 days)
- Short online survey: tasks delegated, time saved, challenges encountered
- Optional submission of a second deliverable

## 6. Assessment rubric for practical deliverable

| Criterion | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Acceptable (2) | Needs revision (1) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Role relevance | Directly supports real work | Relevant to role | Generic but usable | Not relevant |
| Prompt quality | Includes role, context, format and constraints | 3 of 4 elements | 1–2 elements | Vague |
| Human editing | Fully adapted to voice and context | Minor edits needed | Noticeably generic | No personalisation |
| Accuracy & safety | Facts verified; no sensitive data; checklist followed | Minor issues | One significant risk | Unverified or unsafe |
| Reflection | Clear 30-day plan with first step | Plan present but vague | Incomplete | No plan |

## 7. Quality assurance process

1. **Content review cycle:** Program content is reviewed every 6 months or after any major AI tool update, whichever comes first.
2. **Facilitator debrief:** After each delivery, facilitators complete a 15-minute debrief form.
3. **Participant feedback analysis:** Feedback scores and comments are reviewed after every 3 deliveries.
4. **Update log:** All changes to the PMD and commercial assets are logged with date, reason and approver.
5. **Sector validation:** Sector-specific examples are reviewed by at least one subject-matter expert every 12 months.
6. **Data privacy audit:** Once per year, the privacy checklist and tool recommendations are reviewed.

---

## 8. Ethical and safety guardrails

- Participants are instructed never to enter personal, confidential or sensitive data into public AI tools.
- AI is framed as a support tool, not an author or decision-maker.
- All outputs must be verified, edited and approved by a human.
- Participants are encouraged to check their organisation's AI-use policy.
- Facilitators correct misuse live and use mistakes as learning moments.
