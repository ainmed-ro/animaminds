## The problem we solve

| Challenge | Cost of inaction |
|---|---|
| AI feels complex, threatening or inaccessible | Professionals avoid it or use it poorly |

---

## What participants will be able to do

After the program, participants will be able to:
1. Explain what generative AI can and cannot do in their professional context.
2. Identify at least 5 repetitive tasks in their own workflow that AI can accelerate.
3. Use core prompting techniques (role, context, format, constraints, iteration).
4. Evaluate AI outputs for accuracy, bias, tone and suitability.
5. Edit and personalise AI-generated content to reflect their own voice.
6. Apply a simple ethical and data-privacy checklist before using AI.
7. Create at least one practical work asset using AI.
8. Develop a personal 30-day AI-use plan.

## FAQ for clients

**Q: Do participants need technical skills?**
A: No. The program is designed for non-technical professionals.

**Q: Can participants use their own AI tools?**
A: Yes. We recommend free tools such as ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot or Claude.

**Q: Is sensitive data safe during the session?**
A: Yes. We teach participants what data NOT to enter and provide a privacy checklist.

**Q: Will this program make participants dependent on AI?**
A: No. The goal is to keep humans in control and use AI as a support tool.

**Q: Is this program suitable for my entire organisation?**
A: Yes, especially in the on-site or Experience Edition format.

**Q: What is the follow-up after the program?**
A: Participants receive a 30-day email sequence with prompts, reflection questions and resources.
