## Global delivery formats

All programs are available in:

- Online
- La sediul organizației
- Open cohort
- Experience Edition

## Principles

1. One image per program card. No reuse across program cards.
2. No AI-generated training images for programs other than AI Fără Haos.
3. No photos of dinners, parties or social gatherings for professional program positioning.
4. Every public-facing text must state the challenge solved, the audience and the format.
