## Verification of core focus

**Critical thinking, adaptability, creativity and judgement remain the primary focus.**

Evidence:
- Section 1 (Purpose): explicitly about human thinking, learning and deciding in complexity.
- Section 3 (Promise): operationalised around questioning, adapting, generating, judging and combining human insight with technology.
- Section 5 (Learning outcomes): LO1–LO3 critical thinking and bias, LO4 creativity, LO5 adaptability, LO6 judgement, LO7 personal development, LO8 human–AI complementarity.
- Section 7 (Program structure): six modules covering thinking in the AI era, critical thinking tools, bias, creativity, adaptability and personal plan.
- Section 11 (Sector adaptations): every scenario focuses on human judgement, not tool use, communication mechanics, conflict or strategic planning.

**AI usage, communication, conflict management and leadership/change remain in their respective PMDs.**

Evidence:
- Section 10.6 Boundary Matrix explicitly assigns AI tool usage to PMD_001, feedback/listening/communication mechanics to PMD_002, conflict de-escalation/emotional regulation to PMD_003, and strategic decisions/change leadership to PMD_004.
- Module 4 boundary note states PMD_005 does not select strategic direction or lead change.
- Module 5 boundary note states PMD_005 does not teach emotional regulation or de-escalation.
- Troubleshooting guide redirects requests about AI tools, communication, conflict and strategic planning to PMD_001–PMD_004.
