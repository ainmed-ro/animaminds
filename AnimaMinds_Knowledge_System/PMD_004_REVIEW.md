# Internal Review: PMD_004_Busola_Deciziilor

**Review date:** 2026-07-12  
**Reviewer:** Cascade  
**Status:** Draft — PMD_004 not modified; review only

---

## Executive summary

PMD_004_Busola_Deciziilor is a solid first draft that follows the approved master template. It focuses on leadership, prioritisation, decision-making and managing change while avoiding overlap with PMD_001 (AI), PMD_002 (communication mechanics) and PMD_003 (conflict/de-escalation). The sector adaptations are present and the tone is aligned.

Main areas for improvement: stronger differentiation from general leadership training, more explicit decision tools, and a clearer boundary statement with the other three PMDs.

**Overall readiness:** 7 / 10 — good draft, needs some refinement before approval.

## 3. What can a participant do the next day after completing the program?

**Verdict:** Clear.

A participant can:
- Apply the Eisenhower matrix to their task list.
- Use the 6-step decision process on a current decision.
- Communicate a direction more clearly to their team.
- Identify stakeholders and criteria for an upcoming decision.
- Start a 30-day action plan.

**Suggested improvement:** Add a "Next-day clarity sprint" one-pager summarising the core tools.

## 5. Are there overlaps between modules?

**Verdict:** Minor overlap.

- **Module 3 and Module 5:** Both involve making a choice and communicating it. Module 3 is the analysis; Module 5 is the communication.
- **Module 1 and Module 3:** Both touch on defining the decision. Boundary is acceptable.
- **PMD_002 boundary:** Direction communication in Module 5 could drift into communication mechanics if not managed.

**Suggested improvement:** Add facilitator notes clarifying the unique focus of each module.
