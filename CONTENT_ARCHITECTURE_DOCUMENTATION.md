## **DOCUMENT TYPE DEFINITIONS**

### **1. WORKBOOK**
**Purpose:** Participant-facing learning materials
**Audience:** Training participants
**Format:** Interactive, premium design
**Content:** Exercises, templates, note-taking areas

### **2. FACILITATOR GUIDE**
**Purpose:** Trainer instruction and support
**Audience:** Facilitators, trainers
**Format:** Instructional, detailed guidance
**Content:** Scripts, timing, troubleshooting

### **3. CPD PACKAGE**
**Purpose:** Accreditation and professional development documentation
**Audience:** Institutions, CPD bodies, participants
**Format:** Professional, formal documentation
**Content:** Learning outcomes, assessment, evidence

### **4. COMMERCIAL SHEET**
**Purpose:** Sales and marketing materials
**Audience:** Potential clients, organizations
**Format:** Marketing-optimized, persuasive
**Content:** Benefits, ROI, testimonials

### **5. CERTIFICATE**
**Purpose:** Completion recognition
**Audience:** Participants, employers, institutions
**Format:** Official, secure document
**Content:** Achievement verification, credentials

### **6. COMPETENCY RECORD**
**Purpose:** Skills assessment and tracking
**Audience:** Participants, HR departments, institutions
**Format:** Assessment-focused, structured
**Content:** Competency evaluation, evidence

### **7. ONE PAGER**
**Purpose:** Quick program overview
**Audience:** General audience, potential participants
**Format:** Concise, visually engaging
**Content:** Program summary, key benefits

### **8. PRESENTATION**
**Purpose:** Delivery support materials
**Audience:** Facilitators, participants
**Format:** Visual, slide-based
**Content:** Visual aids, structured content

### **9. PILOT MATERIALS**
**Purpose:** Program testing and refinement
**Audience:** Internal team, pilot participants
**Format:** Experimental, iterative
**Content:** Test materials, feedback forms

## **NAMING CONVENTIONS**

### **Folder Naming**
- **Romanian:** `PMD_XXX_Program_Name` (using underscores, no spaces)
- **English:** `PMD_XXX_Program_Name` (using underscores, no spaces)
- **Document Types:** Title case with underscores for multi-word names

### **File Naming Convention**
```
[Program_Code]_[Document_Type]_[Language]_[Version].[Extension]

Examples:
- PMD_002_Workbook_RO_v1.0.docx
- PMD_002_Facilitator_Guide_RO_v1.0.docx
- PMD_002_CPD_Package_RO_v1.0.docx
- PMD_002_Workbook_EN_v1.0.docx
```

### **Version Control**
- **v1.0:** Initial version
- **v1.1:** Minor updates
- **v2.0:** Major revisions
- **Draft:** Working versions
- **Final:** Approved versions
