## **DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS**

### **Typography**
- **Headings:** Montserrat Bold (24-32pt)
- **Subheadings:** Montserrat Semi-Bold (18-24pt)
- **Body:** Lato Regular (12-14pt)
- **Quotes:** Playfair Display Italic (14-16pt)
- **Captions:** Lato Italic (10-11pt)

### **Color Palette**
- **Primary:** #2C3E50 (Deep Blue)
- **Secondary:** #E74C3C (Accent Red)
- **Neutral Dark:** #34495E (Dark Gray)
- **Neutral Medium:** #7F8C8D (Medium Gray)
- **Neutral Light:** #BDC3C7 (Light Gray)
- **Background:** #FFFFFF (White)

### **Layout Standards**
- **Page Margins:** 2.5cm all sides
- **Column Width:** Single column with generous spacing
- **Line Spacing:** 1.5 for body text
- **Paragraph Spacing:** 12pt after paragraphs
- **Section Breaks:** Professional dividers with brand elements

### **Visual Elements**
- **Icons:** Custom professional icon set
- **Dividers:** Professional section breaks
- **Checkboxes:** Custom designed checkboxes
- **Templates:** Structured input areas with subtle guidelines
- **Quotes:** Professional pull-quote styling
