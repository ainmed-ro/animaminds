# Online Delivery Structure Report

**Date:** 2026-07-12
**Scope:** Redesign Online Live delivery around shorter sessions (preferred 2–2.5 h, maximum 3 h).
**Constraint:** Total contact hours and total learning hours from the standardization proposal are preserved.

---

## 1. Design Principles

- Each Online Live session is between **2 h and 3 h**, preferably **2 h to 2.5 h**.
- Total **contact hours** remain equal to the sum of module timings in each PMD.
- Total **learning hours** (contact + individual activities) are unchanged.
- Module groupings are recommendations; facilitators may adjust breaks and pacing within the session limits.
- Where a 6.5 h contact programme cannot be divided cleanly into equal 2 h sessions, one module is split across two sessions.

---

## 2. Consistent Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Contact Hours** | Live, facilitator-led session time. |
| **Individual Activities** | Structured self-study / reflection / worksheets outside live sessions. |
| **Total Learning Hours** | Contact Hours + Individual Activities. |

## 5. Practical Scheduling Recommendations

- **Default spacing:** one session per day (e.g. Monday / Wednesday / Friday) or three consecutive days.
- **Maximum gap:** do not leave more than 7 days between sessions, to maintain momentum.
- **Pre-work:** 15–30 min individual reflection before Session 1 can be added without changing total learning hours, by deducting the same amount from post-session individual activities.
- **Session 3 length:** programmes with a 2.5 h final session can optionally schedule a 10-minute break in the middle.
